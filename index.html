Who we are?

Helpers Touch Initiative is a non-governmental organisation set on empowering women, orphans and widows in our society.

We seek to alleviate poverty and break the vicious circle of redundancy, hopelessness, total dependancy, poor perception of self, poor living condition, exposure to negative informal education and assimilation of nagative principles.
